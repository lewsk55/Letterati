Model notes

Uses the real scoreOf, computeReward, px, seeded shop pool, applyOffer, reroll ladder, pile cycling, and all perk state hooks from the loaded game file, with the real dictionary in this folder. Simplifications: challenges are off; wild vowels play as E and wild consonants as S; clone and copy cat tiles are held but not played; free jazz is never used as a non-word; letter ordering favors Drop Cap and Serif positions but is not fully optimal. Calibration: these bots buy only what the seeded shop actually offers, so absolute win rates run well below the in-game predictor bot (which assumes engine pieces are always purchasable). Treat sim numbers as comparative, not absolute: A/B two builds, compare styles, and watch the purchase and win-correlation table. Bot behavior lives in STYLE_WEIGHTS and LEVELS near the top of the script and is meant to be tuned as you QA.
