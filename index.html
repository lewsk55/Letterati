How to Play

Forge words from the 7 letter slugs in your case. Longer words and rarer letters score more. Set a word and its letters shuffle back into your deck, then you'll draw a fresh 7.

Beat each round's target within your words (turns). Run out short and you go into overtime. Each extra word costs a heart (you have five), keeping your score and hand, until the target falls or your hearts run out.

Stuck with junk? Drop letters on the canvas and tap Discard to swap them (3 a round). The same word can be set as many times as you like.

Made a placement you didn't mean? Tap Undo to step back, one move at a time, right up until you set a word or discard.

Clear a round to earn coins for the print shop: new letters, gilding, perks and more. Beat all 8 rounds to win the press, then push your luck in endless if you dare.

At rounds 3, 5 and 7 you can take an optional challenge: a tougher round in exchange for a reward you pick up front. Clear it to claim the reward. Miss it and you simply drop into normal overtime.
